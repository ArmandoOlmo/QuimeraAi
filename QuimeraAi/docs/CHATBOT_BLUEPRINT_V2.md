# ChatbotBlueprint V2

## ES

`ChatbotBlueprint V2` convierte ChatCore en el contrato canónico del AI Business Agent de Quimera. El blueprint mantiene compatibilidad con los campos legacy (`businessKnowledge`, `productKnowledge`, `policyKnowledge`, `eventIntents`) y añade contratos para identidad del agente, fuentes de conocimiento, acciones, lead capture, handoff, citas, ecommerce, restaurantes, real estate, Bio Page, Finance, canales, pruebas, analytics y deployment.

Reglas de seguridad de CC1:

- Todo lo generado por AI Studio queda en `needs_review`.
- Ninguna acción pública queda `enabled` automáticamente.
- `check_order_status` requiere autenticación/verificación y queda deshabilitado.
- `create_appointment` requiere confirmación, idempotencia, auditoría y validación server-side de disponibilidad.
- `subscribe_email_audience` requiere consentimiento y revisión de supresión.
- `create_finance_quote_request` requiere consentimiento y crea solo borradores en Finance; no crea enlaces de pago, cargos Stripe ni asientos contables.
- Voice queda deshabilitado hasta configurar `agentId` por proyecto.
- Los textos de readiness, fallback y pruebas generados por este contrato incluyen variante `ES:` y `EN:`.

## EN

`ChatbotBlueprint V2` turns ChatCore into Quimera's canonical AI Business Agent contract. The blueprint remains backward-compatible with legacy fields (`businessKnowledge`, `productKnowledge`, `policyKnowledge`, `eventIntents`) and adds contracts for agent identity, knowledge sources, actions, lead capture, handoff, appointments, ecommerce, restaurants, real estate, Bio Page, Finance, channels, testing, analytics, and deployment.

CC1 safety rules:

- Everything generated by AI Studio stays in `needs_review`.
- No public action is automatically `enabled`.
- `check_order_status` requires authentication/verification and remains disabled.
- `create_appointment` requires confirmation, idempotency, audit logging, and server-side availability checks.
- `subscribe_email_audience` requires consent and suppression review.
- `create_finance_quote_request` requires consent and creates only Finance drafts; it does not create payment links, Stripe charges, or ledger entries.
- Voice stays disabled until a per-project `agentId` is configured.
- Readiness, fallback, and test copy generated by this contract includes `ES:` and `EN:` variants.

## Contract / Contrato

- `agentProfile`: bilingual agent behavior, brand voice, welcome, fallback, and escalation copy.
- `knowledgeSources`: structured source inventory with owner module, visibility, health, confidence, freshness, source entity ids, review flags, and source map.
- `actions`: central Action Registry draft with permissions, confirmation, auth, consent, idempotency, audit, status, and readiness.
- `leadCapture`: conversational/pre-chat/exit intent strategy, required fields, tags, scoring, transcript storage, and AI intent analysis.
- `handoff`: reasons, business hours placeholder, inbox, notes, AI summary, suggested replies, and assignment strategy.
- `appointments`, `ecommerce`, `restaurants`, `realEstate`, `bioPage`, `finance`: module-specific capability contracts.
- `channels`: Website, Storefront, Checkout, Bio Page, Booking Page, Restaurant Menu, Realty Property Page, Admin Preview, voice, WhatsApp, SMS, and email readiness.
- `testing`: personas, regression questions, expected actions, blocked-answer rules, and evaluation readiness.
- `analytics`: events, metrics, conversion goals, and attribution requirements.
- `deployment`: deployed surfaces, embed settings, appearance source, voice settings, and safety settings.
